Matric System

Secondary School Programme
Class VI - Class X

SSCA secondary school programme consists of modern educational standards. The school has been registered and recognized long ago by the board of secondary education. A very scientific and latest curriculum in class VI onwards. The faculty for the secondary classes is highly qualified, experienced and dedicated.

Science and Computer Labs
SSCA is equipped with the best school laboratories in Karachi. All of our secondary campuses have a stage of the art computer lab with advanced systems supported by internet connections and a fully equipped science lab, especially designed to cater for educational needs and to excite research abilities in students.

School Term
The School year begins in the first week of April. The academic year is divided into two terms, Namely
a) First Term: April to October
b) Second Term: November to March

Home Work & Class Work
Home work will only be given after a particular topic is explained thoroughly. Class work of the same topic shall be done under the guidance of the teacher. No home work will be given any work that has not been done in the class. Help your children by supervising his home work but never do it for them.

Studies and Private Tuitions
Results are announced half yearly. More information can be obtained from the principal or the vice principal upon request. Kindly note that failure for the second time in the same class leads to dismissal.
The school authorities discourage private tuitions. No private tutor should be engaged without obtaining prior permission No school teacher shall act as a tutor to a student of her own class unless the Principal grants permission.
